Biography

I am currently a final year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science of Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), supervised by Prof. Qing Wang. My research interests lie in 3D vision and computational photography, with a particular focus on Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF), Neural Camera Models, and 3D AI-Generated Content (AIGC). From 2021 to 2023, I worked as a research intern at the Visual Computing Center, Tencent AI Lab, under the mentorship of Qi Zhang. I also interned with the 3D Vision and Computational Photography Group at Tsinghua University, guided by Prof. Yebin Liu. I was a research intern at Shanghai AI Lab, collaborating with Tengfei Wang and Ziwei Liu. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science, NPU, in 2020.
